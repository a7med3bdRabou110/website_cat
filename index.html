About CAT Reloaded?

CAT" is a non-profit foundation focused on"

CAT's primary goal was to create an IT community in Mansoura University that is dynamic, innovative and highly scalable for the undergraduate computer engineering/science students, that would help them achieve a good level of technical proficiency through projects and seminars.
